About Me

I am a full stack developer from Selma, CA. A small but growing city south of Fresno. The very thought of being able to create things that can help people achieve their goals or dreams is why I love being a developer. From small simple ideas to big complicated ideas, I love making them come to life.

Python

The demand for Python developers has increased in the past few years. Python is my second language but it is my preferred language. I can build web apps using Flask or Django.

I'm currently taking a course on becoming a Data Scientist along with Java.

JavaScript

JavaScript is the language that powers the web. No wonder the demand for JavaScript Developers has increased significantly with the advent of React, Vue, and Angular 6.

AngularJS was one of my first frameworks but I currently updating my skills to work with ReactJS.

Databases

SQL Databases are among my specialties. I love working with data. From small databases that utilize the like of SQLite for Native running apps or big complicated databases that need many tables to store related information, or the modern NoSQl Databases like MongoDB, I can build them.
